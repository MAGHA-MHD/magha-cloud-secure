Security Information

Encryption

Encryption is the process of converting information or data into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access. We use strong encryption methods to protect sensitive information.

Data Protection

We are committed to protecting the personal data of our users. Since the site collects user data, we follow strict protocols to secure stored data and ensure it is only accessible to authorized personnel.

Security Measures

Our security measures include:

  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Firewall protection
  • Two-factor authentication for user accounts
  • Data backup and disaster recovery plans.

We continuously monitor our systems for vulnerabilities and threats to ensure the highest level of security.
